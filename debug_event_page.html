2nd Annual Smart Ports & Logistics Transformation Summit KSA 2025

15 - 16 Sep, 2025 from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM (GMT+3)
Jeddah
REGISTER
ADD TO CALENDAR ▼

Email: alister@verve-management.com

The 2nd Annual Smart Ports & Logistics Transformation Summit KSA 2025, will be an unparalleled two-day conference set to take place in the vibrant city of Jeddah to discuss strategies, innovation, and technologies vital in achieving the KSA Vision 2030 to emerge as a logistics hub in the MENA region.

Saudi Arabia, one of the largest maritime trading nations in the world valuing over USD500 billion, transporting over 90% of its imports and exports is set for transformation. The Saudi Arabia Freight and Logistics Market is estimated over $24 billion in 2024, with a promising trajectory to reach $32.88 billion by 2029 at a CAGR of 5.52%.
